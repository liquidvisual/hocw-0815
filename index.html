HOCW Layers - Melbourne CBD & Eastern Suburbs

HOCW is an established and leading mid-size law firm with experienced and accredited lawyers delivering a range of corporate and private client legal services.

Our clients include corporate clients across of range of industries, professionals, SMEs, the self- employed, family businesses, families, couples and individuals.

  • Commercial & Corporate Law
  • Commercial Litigation & Dispute Resolution
  • Building & Construction
  • Owners Corporation
  • Franchises
  • Employment & Workplace Relations

HOCW's Commercial practice has a particular focus on Business, Succession Law, Franchises, Employment, Intellectual Property, Superannuation, Leasing – commercial & retail, Owners Corporations, Building & Construction, Domestic Building Disputes, Insolvency, and Consumer Law.

  • Wills & Estates
  • Family Law
  • Property & Conveyancing

Our Private Law practice provides families, couples and individuals with the legal services including Wills & Estates, Family Law, Conveyancing, and Aged Care & Retirement Living.
